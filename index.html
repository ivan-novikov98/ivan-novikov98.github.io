I am a temporary teaching and research assistant at Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne. I also have an M.Sc. in Optimization from Université Paris-Saclay (supervisor Guillaume Vigeral) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from Higher School of Economics (supervisor Mikhail Skopenkov).

Research interests: zero-sum stochastic games, in discrete and in continuous time; asymptotic value in zero-sum stochastic games.
