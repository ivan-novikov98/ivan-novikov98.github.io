Ivan Novikov's homepage

Starting from September 2026, I will be a postdoctoral fellow at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, working under the supervision of Xiao Li. Previously, I held positions as a Temporary Teaching and Research Assistant (ATER) at Université Paris Dauphine-PSL and Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne. I defended my PhD thesis in applied mathematics at Université Paris Dauphine-PSL, which I completed while based at CEREMADE under the supervision of Guillaume Vigeral. I also hold an M.Sc. in Optimization from Université Paris-Saclay (supervisor Guillaume Vigeral) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Higher School of Economics (supervisor Mikhail Skopenkov).

Research interests: game theory and optimization.
